# Lifestyle B-Roll

General solo-travel lifestyle clips not tied to a specific port, ship, or sailor. Used as filler in marketing pages, social transitions, and homepage section accents.

Examples: a person at a railing watching the sunset, friends laughing over dinner, packing a suitcase, sunrise from a balcony, cocktail being poured at a beach bar.

Naming: `<vibe>-01.mp4`, e.g. `sunset-railing-01.mp4`, `cocktail-pour-01.mp4`.

Specs in master README. **Stay under 24 MB per file.**
