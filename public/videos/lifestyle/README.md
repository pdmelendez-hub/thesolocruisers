# Lifestyle B-Roll

General solo-travel lifestyle clips not tied to a specific port, ship, or sailor. Used as section accents, social transitions, and marketing montages.

Naming: `lifestyle-<topic>.mp4` (e.g. `lifestyle-sunset-ocean-walk.mp4`, `lifestyle-planning-map.mp4`).

See `../INVENTORY.md` for the current list. Specs in master `../README.md`. **Stay under 24 MB per file.**
