# Social Cuts — Reels & Stories (9:16 vertical)

Pre-cut vertical assets ready to drop into Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Stories. These do **not** ship to the website by default — they live here as the canonical source of truth so the marketing team has one place to grab them.

| Subfolder | Aspect | Length | Audio | Use |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `reels-9x16/` | 1080×1920 | 15–60 s | Yes (music or VO) | Reels, TikTok, polished |
| `stories-9x16/` | 1080×1920 | 6–15 s | Optional | Stories, ephemeral, lower polish |

Naming: `<campaign>-reel-01.mp4`, e.g. `friendships-reel-01.mp4`, `virgin-scarlet-night-reel-01.mp4`.

Specs in master README. **Stay under 24 MB per file** — even though these may not deploy, keeping them small helps the repo stay healthy.
