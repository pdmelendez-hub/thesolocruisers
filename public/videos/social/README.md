# Social Cuts — Reels & Stories (9:16 vertical)

Pre-cut vertical assets ready to drop into Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Stories. These do **not** ship to the website by default — they live here as the canonical source of truth so the marketing team has one place to grab them.

Naming: `reel-<topic>.mp4` (polished, 15–60 s, with audio) or `story-<topic>.mp4` (ephemeral, 6–15 s).

Examples: `reel-friendships.mp4`, `reel-virgin-scarlet-night.mp4`, `story-pov-departure.mp4`.

Specs: 1080×1920 H.264 MP4 with audio. **Stay under 24 MB per file** — even though these may not deploy, it keeps the repo healthy.
